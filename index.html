How family/friend access works:
Each person logs in with their own Google account. Their data lives in their own Google Drive. You (admin) get read access to their Spendly file to view their dashboard — they see only their own data.

You'll generate an access link. They open it, log in with Google, and their data becomes visible in your admin view.
